How to get price data
Use the Google Finance formula in Google Sheets to pull monthly close prices, then download as CSV and upload below.
1
Paste this formula into a Google Sheet (replace SYMBOL)
=GOOGLEFINANCE("SYMBOL","close",DATE(2020,1,1),TODAY(),"WEEKLY")
Examples: RELIANCE.NSE · AAPL · 0P0000XVPO.BO (for MFs on BSE)
2
Download the sheet as CSV
File → Download → Comma Separated Values (.csv)
3
Upload against the matching holding below
Upload weekly data only. The CSV must have date and close columns.
